Comic Book Letterer — MVP

1. Click Load Page Image to load comic art
2. Provide a script by one of three methods:
   • Upload Photo(s) of script pages → AI reads and extracts dialogue
   • Paste script text → click Parse Script (regex) or AI Parse (any format)
3. Click Script View in the toolbar to see your script photo and comic page side by side
4. Drag dialogue chips onto the page, or click + Add Balloon to place an empty balloon
5. Drag balloons to move, drag corners to resize, drag the blue tail tip to point at a character
6. Click a balloon to edit its text, font, size, and colors in the right inspector
